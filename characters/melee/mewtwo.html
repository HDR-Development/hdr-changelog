Moveset

  • General
    • The outermost two tail hurtboxes are only present if performing a tail-based attack

  • Double Jump:
    • Release Jump within three frames of performing an aerial to interrupt the vertical momentum of the jump (double jump cancel), or continue holding Jump to rise with the aerial

  • Float:
    • Mewtwo has an omnidirectional float that can be held for up to 60 frames (can only move up/down after frame 3)
      • There is a 2 frame leniency window at the beginning of a double jump where floating will not take away the jump
      • Float can be used out of tumble and platdrop
